What is it?

Most basically, it is a team of people under the age of 30 years old, who are joining hands as a team, each agreeing to give $30 month in committed monthly support to Nipawin Bible College by April 30, 2014.

Significance

30under30 increases our annual Many Hands contributions by nearly $11,000. Additionally, and perhaps more importantly, it significantly grows the base of young givers we'd like to see as part of our NBC support team!
